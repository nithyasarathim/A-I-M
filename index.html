Academic Insights & Mentoring

Academic Insight & Mentorship is a web platform designed to help students improve their coding skills and guide them in choosing the right career path. The platform tracks student progress on coding sites like CodeChef, Codeforces, HackerRank, and LeetCode, and ranks them on a leaderboard within their class. It also uses AI to suggest personalized career paths and connects students with alumni mentors for career guidance and interview preparation.

Purpose
  • Motivate students to improve their coding skills through friendly competition.
  • Provide personalized career guidance based on student interests and skills.
  • Connect students with mentors to support their career development.
